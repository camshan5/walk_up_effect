Heal yourself

Your role. Be courageous. Know that you are not alone. Walk Up and express your struggles.

Result. Asking for support shows strength and can encourage others around you to do the same. When you ask for help you are starting your journey to living a happier life.

1

Recognize

Notice the signs and see the struggle that you may be facing.

2

Be Humble

Its ok to have a struggle and its ok to ask for support.

3

Ask for support

Be courageous. Walk Up to someone for support.

4

Listen

Listen to their advice.

5

Take Action

Make time in each day to work on yourself.

6

Surround

Surround yourself with people that support you.
