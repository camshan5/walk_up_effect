Our Mission Our Goals

We are a do-good company.

Walk Ups mission is simple... to bring hope, joy, and courage to those who need it. Everyone has struggles and everyone has obstacles they are trying to overcome. We firmly believe that sometimes someone may just need to hear the right message to feel comfortable asking for help or to see the importance of being the support someone may be seeking.

We were founded with the goal to normalize and spark the conversation behind mental health and encourage people to talk and share their struggles and story with others. We want everyone to feel comfortable asking for support. We hope to make an impact on every staff member, student, and parent that takes part in our program. It is important to us that every student we come into contact with feels inspired to support others, has the strength to get through another day, and wants to be the change we need in the world.

Our primary goal: Impact.

We hope to make an impact on every individual that takes part in our program. It is important to us that every group we come into contact with feels inspired to help others, has the strength to get through another day, and wants to be the change we need in the world.

Our goal is to change the perception of the way people view mental health. We want those who are struggling to feel comfortable asking for help and sharing their story. And we want the others to be able to notice the signs of those struggling with mental health and be courageous and willing to be the support they may need.
