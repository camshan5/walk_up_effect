Motivate

We believe in the power of story and we believe it is tough to judge someone when you know their story.

When a struggling individual hears someone share their story on mental health issues and how they continue to overcome it, they too may feel motivated to speak up and share their story which in turn can motivate more and more people.

With our program and fundraising platform, your school will be provided with an amazing motivational speaker that will share their powerful mental health story to increase strength and inspire individuals to walk up.
